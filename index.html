Hi, I’m James.

I do strategy and communications for social movements and progressive politics.

I live and work in Melbourne on the stolen lands of the Wurundjeri people of the Kulin nation. I grew up in Shepparton in Northern Victoria on stolen Yorta Yorta land.

I've worked in politics, social movements and non-profits as a strategist, communicator & digital systems architect for over a decade. I lead teams, develop strategies and manage projects. I use digital tools to organise groups, mobilise movements and build collective power. I use words to craft stories and narratives that unite supporters, communicate ideas and inspire action.

I run a newsletter about the climate crisis, democracy and social movements called Infinite Scroll.

I'm currently the Executive Director at Digital Rights Watch.

I was a co-founder of Tomorrow Movement and an alumni of 350.org's global digital team.

I’ve done a whole bunch of other work too.
